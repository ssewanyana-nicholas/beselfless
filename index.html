SELFLESS Begins With Self

We believe that the way to build stronger families and communities starts with individuals. Our mission is to empower people with the tools and resources to become self-sufficient. SELFLESS aims to offer these resources through our various financial need based programs. We focus on education and self improvement as the keys to self-sufficiency. SELFLESS really does begin with "self"

Frequently Asked Questions

Beselfless Uganda is a non-profit organization that aims to empower young single adults to become self-sufficient through online education offered by BYU Pathway Worldwide and self-improvement. We focus on education and self improvement as the keys to self-sufficiency. BeSELFLESS really does begin with "self"

Beselfless is open to all young single adults between 18-30 years, who are willing to learn and grow through online education offered by BYU Pathway Worldwide and self-improvement. You can join by filling out the application form on our website.

We offer tuition to qualifying individuals and a weekly stipend to facilitate students for a smooth duration in school. We also offer rewards to outstanding and best performing students.

Joining Besslelfless is free of charge. However, you must be a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints or a close friend to the members of Beselfless, and must maintain a high standard of conduct and behavior.
